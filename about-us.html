Our Story

Nearly Four Decades of
Community Excellence

Since 1987, we've grown alongside Central Texas, adapting to the evolving needs of homeowners associations while staying true to our core values.

1987

Where It All Began

PS Property Management was founded with a clear vision: to bring professional, ethical management services to Central Texas homeowners associations. From day one, integrity and personal service were the cornerstones of our approach.

1990s

Growing With Austin

As the Austin area experienced rapid growth, so did we. We expanded our portfolio to serve communities across Williamson and Travis counties, building a reputation for reliability and transparent financial management.

2000s

Embracing Technology

We made a pivotal investment in the most robust technology platform in the industry, giving board members and homeowners 24/7 access to community information, real-time reporting, and streamlined communication tools.

2010s

Two Offices, One Mission

To better serve our growing portfolio of communities, we opened our South Austin location at 8701 Menchaca Rd, complementing our Round Rock headquarters. Two offices means faster response times and more personal service across Central Texas.

Today

100+ Years Combined Experience

Our management team brings more than a century of combined expertise in community association management, finance, construction, compliance, and customer service. Every team member is dedicated to continuing education and Texas HOA law.

The Future

Leading the Way Forward

We continue to invest in AI-powered tools, real-time dashboards, and automated systems that make community management more transparent, efficient, and responsive than ever before. The best is still ahead.

Our communities are more than just properties — they're homes where families build memories, neighbors become friends, and every day brings a sense of belonging.
