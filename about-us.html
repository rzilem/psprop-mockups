About PS Property Management

Serving Central Texas communities with integrity, transparency, and technology since 1987.

Welcome to PS Property Management

Since 1987, PS Property Management has been dedicated to serving homeowners associations and condominium communities throughout Central Texas. As a locally owned and operated company, we understand the unique needs of Austin-area communities and the importance of transparent, responsive management.

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional service that strengthens communities, protects property values, and gives residents peace of mind. We believe that great HOA management combines deep local expertise with cutting-edge technology, all delivered with a personal touch.

Our History

PS Property Management was founded in 1987 with a vision to bring professional, ethical management services to Central Texas homeowners associations. Over nearly four decades, we've grown alongside the Austin community, adapting to the evolving needs of HOAs while staying true to our core values of integrity, transparency, and accountability.

What started as a small local operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted HOA management firms. Today, we serve communities across the greater Austin area from our two convenient locations in Round Rock and South Austin, bringing more than 100 years of combined management experience to every community we serve.

What Makes Us Different

Technology That Works for You

We've invested heavily in the most robust technology platform in the industry, giving board members and homeowners 24/7 access to community information, streamlined communication tools, and real-time reporting. Our systems automate routine tasks so our team can focus on what matters most: serving your community.

Transparency in Everything We Do

We believe homeowners and board members deserve complete visibility into their community's operations. From detailed financial reporting to clear communication about maintenance and compliance issues, we lead with transparency at every level.

Local Expertise, Personal Service

As a locally owned Austin company, we're not just managing properties — we're serving our neighbors. We understand Central Texas communities, local regulations, and the unique challenges HOAs face in our rapidly growing region. When you call, you'll speak with someone who knows your community by name.

Proven Track Record

With 72 reviews across our Round Rock and South Austin offices, our reputation speaks for itself. But we never take that for granted — we work every day to earn your trust.

Our Team

Our management team brings diverse expertise in community association management, finance, construction, compliance, and customer service. Every team member is committed to continuing education and staying current with industry best practices and Texas HOA law.

What unites us is a genuine passion for helping communities thrive. We're problem-solvers, communicators, and advocates for the residents we serve.

Community Commitment

We're proud to be part of the Central Texas community, and we believe in giving back. Beyond managing associations, we're committed to supporting local initiatives, staying engaged with industry organizations, and contributing to the ongoing conversation about best practices in community management.

Our communities are more than just properties on a balance sheet — they're homes, gathering places, and neighborhoods where families build memories. That's a responsibility we take seriously.
